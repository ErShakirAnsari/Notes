# Maven - Notes

### How to add a local jar to your maven project:

<dependency>
   <groupId>nsdl.esign</groupId>
   <artifactId>nsdl-app</artifactId>
   <version>1.0</version>
   <scope>system</scope>
  <systemPath>${basedir}/lib/nsdle_sign_v1.4.jar</systemPath>
</dependency>

${basedir} is location where is your pom.xml file
